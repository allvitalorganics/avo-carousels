01 · The Pivot

Why v1 missed · why v2 leads with confession

v1 led with the brand's category position ("anti-magic-pill"). That's a position, not a hook. People buy identities, not positions — and they buy from founders who admit they're human, not from brands who declare their thesis.

v1 Hook
"다들 마법의 알약을 찾아요. 그런 건 없어요." — declarative, answers the question before opening it. Brain auto-categorizes as ad.
v2 Hook
"솔직히 말하면, 저도 매일 채소 350g 못 먹어요." — founder confession with Naver-verified stat. Curiosity gap: wait, the founder can't either? Locks in the next 5 seconds.
v1 Story
No protagonist. Abstract category positioning throughout ("Everyone's stacking, we're cleaner"). Reader stays in analytical mode.
v2 Story
Alex is protagonist all 60 seconds. His confession, his counter, his morning, his ritual. Real motions. Documentary register. Stories beat abstractions 9-to-1.
v1 Identity
Beat 3 dumps specs: "18종 / 5,000mg / 단백질 5g / 4종 무첨가" — pure Layer 1 (product attributes). Viewer never sees themselves.
v2 Identity
Identity Layer 3 stamped in Beat 3: "매일 하나만 바꾸는 사람." Viewer recognizes themselves before specs ever appear. Specs demoted to Beat 4 footnote.
v1 Authority
Daoom heritage saved for Ep 02 tease. Ep 01 itself has zero credential anchor on first watch.
v2 Authority
One-second flash of Daoom Factory 1988 archive photo at Beat 4, with "3대째 / 김수경 박사" small-text overlay. Restrained, fast, on-screen once. Heritage = backstop, not centerpiece.

02 · The Thesis (revised)

One sentence the brand stands behind

Core Message · v2

매일 채소 350g — 사실 저도 못 먹어요. 그래서 만들었어요. 마법은 없고요, 하루의 첫 결정 하나 챙기는 거예요.

"350g of vegetables a day — honestly, I can't either. So I made this. No magic — just one first decision of the day, owned."

What the viewer walks away with

1 · The founder is honest. He admits he can't do the 350g either. That makes the brand human.

2 · Identity stamp: "I'm someone who changes one thing in the morning." The viewer joins a movement, not a transaction.

3 · There's a real product. 18 organic vegetables, family-made since 1988. Heritage is shown, not claimed.

4 · Series continues. Episode 2 already promised at the end.

06 · Voice Rules

Do this · Don't do this

Do

  • Open with confession, not declaration
  • 2세대 register · self-talk · -요/-대요/-음 mix
  • Real motions, real timing · pour, sip, set down
  • Documentary register · side profile · NOT eye-contact-to-camera
  • Let silence and the kettle carry beats
  • End with brand line, not CTA
  • Heritage flash · 1 second · once
  • Identity stamp on screen at Beat 03

Don't

  • NO founder glamour shot to camera with product
  • NO grandfather/Daoom as centerpiece
  • NO "혁신적인" / "최고의" / "프리미엄"
  • NO price, NO discount, NO "link in bio"
  • NO trending TikTok sound or transition
  • NO mention of Pheeew, Bobsnu, SO
  • NO before/after weight or therapeutic claims
  • NO 400g WHO stat — use 350g 한국영양학회 (Korean MFDS)
